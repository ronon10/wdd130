Exploring the World - Site Plan

Site Name

Exploring the World - The name reflects the site's purpose, which is to share information about popular travel destinations, including details on local currency and conversion to US dollars.

Site Purpose

To provide useful information about popular tourist destinations, including attractions, travel tips, and a currency converter to assist travelers in planning their trips.

Scenarios

  • What are the main tourist attractions in Paris?
  • How much is 100 euros in US dollars?

Color Scheme

Blue (#0077b6) - Used for titles and highlight elements.

White (#ffffff) - Main background for better readability.

Light Gray (#f8f9fa) - For secondary sections.

Typography

Montserrat - Used for titles.

Open Sans - Used for body text.
